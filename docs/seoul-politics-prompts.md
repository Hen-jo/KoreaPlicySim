# Seoul Politics Prompt Pack

These prompts are intended for the `korea_society_policy` preset and are designed to keep early runs focused and interpretable.

## Prompt 1: Seoul mayoral housing pledge

```text
Simulate how voters in Seoul respond when a mayoral candidate announces a district-differentiated housing pledge that combines redevelopment easing in Gangnam, rental protection in Gwanak, and youth housing expansion in Mapo. Focus on approval shifts, support movement by district, media framing, and whether undecided voters become more favorable or more skeptical.
```

## Prompt 2: District-level transport policy

```text
Simulate how residents in Seoul react when a major transport policy promises new subway access and bus network reform for eastern and southwestern districts. Compare how office workers, older homeowners, young renters, small business owners, and parents respond in different districts, and identify where the policy improves support and where it triggers distrust.
```

## Prompt 3: Candidate scandal and recovery

```text
Simulate how a Seoul local election changes after a leading candidate faces a corruption-related controversy and responds with a policy-heavy recovery message. Track favorability changes, support erosion or recovery by constituency, media amplification, and whether swing voters in competitive districts move back toward the candidate or toward opponents.
```

## Minimum upload set

- One candidate pledge or policy brief
- One polling or approval summary
- Two or three news/commentary articles
- One district issue summary or civic group statement
